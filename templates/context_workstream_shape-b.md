> When to use this shape
> Shape B organizes a workstream into categorical buckets (decisions, in-progress items, blockers, deliverables, next actions) rather than a session-by-session log. Reach for it on decision-heavy or pipeline-heavy work, where several threads move at once and the point is to see current state at a glance, not replay how you got there. This is a domain-driven choice, not an enforced one: if the workstream is really one thread of iterative debugging, switch to Shape A (session log) instead.
